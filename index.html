WHAT IS SWIMMING FOR YOU?

Mission

Promote health, well-being and sports to the public

Goal

Our goal in Swimming For You is to allow everybody around the world to have the oppurtunity to recieve professional feedback on their swimming.

How it works

You will post a video of you swimming which stroke you would want to improve. People with Swimming For You accounts will provide you with feedback.

Rewarding System

You may be thinking... why would people give me feedback? This is because we have a rewarding system. People earn points by providing feedback to the video you posted. The amount of points earned is decided by the rating you gave the feedback.

Once you reach a certain amount of points, you can claim various rewards such as gift cards.

Rewarding System

The rewarding system is the key system that allows the "Swimming For You" website to function properly.

By earning points from ratings of the recipents of your advice, you can earn various prizes to help you improve your health, well-being and enthusiasm for swimming.

These are some of the big prizes you can get:

Tech Swimswuit

$100 Walmart Giftcard
